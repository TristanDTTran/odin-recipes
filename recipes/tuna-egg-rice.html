Tuna, Eggs and Rice

Tuna, Eggs and Rice, my go-to quick lunch!

This recipe can be made in many different ways whether simple or more complex.

Sometimes a little goes a long way. And today we are going to prepare Tuna, Eggs and Rice the simple way.

Ingredients

  • Canned tuna
  • Boiled eggs
  • Soy sauce
  • Chilli oil
  • Cooked rice
  • Sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Cook rice
  2. Boil eggs
  3. Serve rice into a bowl
  4. Peel eggs
  5. Put tuna and boiled eggs into rice bowl
  6. Serve with soy sauce, chilli oil and sesame
  7. Enjoy!
