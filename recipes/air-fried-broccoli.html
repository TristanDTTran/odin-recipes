Air fried broccoli Recipe

(Return home)

Air fried broccoli are so easy and simple!

This recipe can be made in many different ways whether simple or more complex.

Sometimes a little goes a long way. And today we are going to prepare Air fried broccoli the simple way.

Ingredients

  • Broccoli
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Wash broccoli and and chop to prepare for air fryer
  2. Line air fryer with baking paper and place broccoli inside
  3. Sprinkle over salt and pepper and olive oil
  4. Air fry for 15 minutes at 175 degrees celsius
  5. Serve onto plate and enjoy!
