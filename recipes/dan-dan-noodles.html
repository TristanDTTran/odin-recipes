Dan Dan Noodles Recipe

Dan Dan noodles are so easy and simple!

This recipe can be made in many different ways whether simple or more complex.

Sometimes a little goes a long way. And today we are going to prepare Dan Dan Noodles the simple way.

Ingredients

  • Wheat noodles
  • Mince pork
  • Soy sauce
  • Chilli oil
  • Black vinegar
  • Ground sichuan pepper
  • Ginger
  • Spring onion

Steps

  1. Mix togher: soy sauce, chilli oil, and vinegar in a bowl
  2. Cook your pork with the ground sichuan pepper
  3. Boil your noodles
  4. Add cooked noodles inside sauce boil
  5. Sprinkle pork over top of noodles
  6. Garnish with chopped spring onion
  7. Enjoy!
