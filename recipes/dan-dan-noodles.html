Dan Dan Noodles Recipe

Dan Dan noodles are so easy and simple!

This recipe can be made in many different ways whether simple or more complex.

Sometimes a little goes a long way. And today we are going to prepare Dan Dan Noodles the simple way.

Ingredients

  • Wheat noodles
  • Mince pork
  • Soy sauce
  • Chilli oil
  • Black vinegar
  • Ground sichuan pepper
  • Ginger

Steps

  1. Wheat noodles
  2. Mince pork
  3. Soy sauce
  4. Chilli oil
  5. Black vinegar
  6. Ground sichuan pepper
  7. Ginger
